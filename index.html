Welcome to
TheScaleyGuitar

Hello and welcome to TheScaleyGuitar! This is site dedicated to keeping the aspiring guitarrist inspired! This site is constantly evolving, with different scales being added as I learn them and fully understand them. What I have done is included the fretboard which shows the scale itself, then I will give you a little theory on the the scale. They are also know as a 'MODE', so if you see that word, know you know what is meant exactly. There is also a small section that will be added in based upon favorite guitar type as well as favorite genre.
